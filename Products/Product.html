Sorts

  • Adygei is a soft whey cheese with a sour-milk taste and a loose consistency.
  • Almette - fresh (similar to homemade cheese) German cheese with additives - greens, garlic.
  • Altenburger is a German soft cheese
  • Alpidamer is an Austrian semi-hard cheese with fairly large holes.
  • Alpinland is an Austrian semi-hard cheese in a yellow wax shell, with a delicate taste.
  • Alpsirler is an Austrian semi-hard cheese with a red crust.
  • Amadeus is an Austrian semi-hard cheese of bright yellow color in a white shell with a relief image of a cross.
  • Anari is a whey cheese made in Cyprus.
  • Anfotyros is a Greek dry unpasteurized cheese made from sheep's milk.
  • Aperifre is fresh, similar to homemade cheese, which is made in the form of small circles that are convenient to pierce with a skewer. Aperitifs are served with aperitif drinks.
  • Aseda is a Swedish hard cheese.
  • Atlet is an Estonian hard cheese with a sour taste.
  • Aura[en] is Finnish blue cheese
